TREASURE

TREASURE (트레저) is a 12-member K-Pop boy group under YG Entertainment. They were formed through a survival show called YG Treasure Box, which was aired back in 2018. TREASURE is comprised of Choi Hyunsuk, Park Jihoon, Yoshi, Kim Junkyu, Mashiho, Yoon Jaehyuk, Asahi, Bang Yedam, Kim Doyoung, Haruto, Park Jeongwoo, and So Junghwan. They were originally supposed to be branded as TREASURE 13, unfortunately, ex-member Ha Yoonbin left the group due to the company's desired music route for the group. He felt to be more suited to be a soloist rather than in a group.

(From L-R, From the top: Kim Junkyu, Yoon Jaehyuk, Park Jeongwoo, Ha Yoonbin, Yoshi, Haruto, Mashiho, Choi Hyunsuk, Park Jihoon, Asahi, Bang Yedam, Kim Doyoung, So Junghwan)

4 out of 12 TREASURE members are Japanese: Yoshi, Mashiho, Asahi, and Haruto. The rest of the members are Korean.

Despite YG Treasure Box being aired on 2018-2019, TREASURE's debut was delayed up until August 7, 2020 due to scandals that involved the company. They debuted with their hit song 'BOY', which earned them numerous awards despite only being rookies at the time.

The members' birthyears range from 1999 to 2005.

From oldest to youngest: Choi Hyunsuk, Park Jihoon, Yoshi, Kim Junkyu, Mashiho, Yoon Jaehyuk, Asahi, Bang Yedam, Kim Doyoung, Haruto, Park Jeongwoo, then So Junghwan.
